# Privacy Policy — Daily Tabs Launcher

Effective date: 2025-12-07

Daily Tabs Launcher is a Chrome extension that helps you open your daily work URLs quickly. We are committed to user privacy and data minimization.

## Data We Store
- Groups and URLs you manually add.
- Storage location: Chrome storage sync (with automatic fallback to Chrome storage local). Data remains in your browser profile.

## Data We Do Not Collect
- No personal data, browsing history, analytics, or telemetry.
- No data is sent to external servers or third parties.
- No ads or tracking.

## Permissions and Purpose
- `storage`: Save and retrieve your groups/URLs.
+- `tabs`: Open the URLs you saved in new tabs. We do not read or modify tab contents.

## Data Sharing and Selling
- We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party.

## Security
- Data relies on Chrome’s storage mechanisms; no additional servers are involved.

## User Control
- You can add, edit, reorder, or delete stored URLs and groups at any time via the options page.
- Uninstalling the extension removes its stored data from Chrome.
